Art Club

Students who enjoy art and want to continue their practice of art should come down room 307, in the green mile, after school, around 3:20pm. Students can create 1-3 but members of Art Club must pay the $5.00 dues.

Asset Club

Assets club helps you to make a difference in your school and community. Assets Club meets every other Tuesday in room 164 at 7:30am. Check the daily bulletin for our next meeting date or see Mrs. Brady or Mrs. Zohlen in the Guidance Office.

Band

Band is composed of: Varsity Marching, Freshman Marching, Symphonic, Concert, Pep & Jazz Band, Color Guard, Wind & Percussion Ensembles, Solo & Ensemble Contests. The next Booster Meeting will be on Monday, Oct. 19 at 7:00pm in the Band Room!

Bowling Club

Millard West Bowling has been involved in the Metro High School Bowling scene since 1995. Practice happens on Wednesday's at Western Bowl from 3:45pm-5:00pm from September to February.

Captivated

Captivated is a wonderful new Bible study and prayer group. Students who want to take their religious understandings further should attend these meetings before school on Thursdays before school.
