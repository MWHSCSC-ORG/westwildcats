Millard West
Club and Activity Resources



This website was established by a former Computer Science teacher, Mr. Chris Ramey. After leaving Millard West in 2013, Mr. Ramey continues to provide our school with the website. Today, the website is maintained and updated by the Millard West Computer Science Club. Originally, this site was only really used for the Pawprint News team at Millard West to provide an online news resource for the school. However as of 2015, the site underwent a major cosmetic and structural reform. Now the site is designed and equipped to become a resource for Millard West's students, staff, and parents.

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback for this site or its content, please contact Mr. Young (Room 204) or members of the Millard West Computer Science Club. We'd love to hear what you have to say.

Thank You!
